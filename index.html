How It Works

Infinite Opportunities

  • Contributions

    Accredited educators from around the globe submit their original worksheets, test practice, and other study materials to our site.

  • Machine Learning

    Our algorithms sift through submitted study materials, providing each student with a daily customized study package.

  • Online Study

    Students fill out a questionnaire and are automatically matched with study materials that are a best fit for them, accessed directly online.
